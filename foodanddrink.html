Food, drink & dining guide

Abergavenny is the home of the famous annual Food and Drink Festival, held every September. This attracts world famous chefs demonstrating their cooking skills and inspiring their audiences.

Restaurants

Monmouthshire is rightly famous for extending a hospitable welcome to all visitors and for the fabulous food on offer. The following restaurants are only a short distance from Trevyr Barn:

  • The Walnut Tree, Llandewi Skirrid
  • The Hardwick
  • Toi Et Moi
  • 1861, Cross Ash
  • Stone Mill
  • The Angel, Abergavenny
  • The Bell at Skenfrith

Friendly local Pubs

In the centre of Grosmont village is The Angel Inn, our friendly local, offering lunches and evening meals and a great selection of local ales and ciders.

Other local pubs offering great food and drink include: The Moon at Garway, The Hunter's Moon at Llangatock Lingoed, The Cottage of Content (Carey), The Crown at Pantygelli and the Bridge Inn at Kentchurch.

Food shopping

Monmouth is justly proud of the fantastic food and drink produced locally. We hope that our visitors will enjoy sampling what's on offer. The Made in Monmouthshire website provides plenty of information about local producers of food, drink, arts and crafts.
