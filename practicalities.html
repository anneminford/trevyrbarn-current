The practicalities

Location & Travel

Grosmont is within easy driving distance of London, Cardiff, the Midlands and Manchester. It is well serviced by local and national train services via Abergavenny, Hereford and Newport (London-Swansea main line). A taxi from Abergavenny station costs £30 - £35.

Public transport facilities in and around Grosmont are fairly limited; information can be found here

Trevyr Barn is located just over a mile to the south west of Grosmont, We will be pleased to provide full travel information and directions upon booking.

Welcome pack

We include a welcome pack containing locally produced goodies . A basket of logs is also provided.

Pets

We are sorry, but we are unable to accommodate pets due to livestock on neighbouring fields.

Linen and towels

Quality bed linen and towels are included.

Parking

There is parking for two cars on a gravel area directly outside the barn, next to the lawned garden, and secure storage for bicycles.

Smoking

We have a no smoking policy inside the barn.
