How we help the environment

  • We optimized the overall energy efficiency rating when we undertook the conversion in 2008 (taking into account the age and construction of the original building).
  • The barn is heated by an energy efficient condensing boiler, and we have fitted the latest thermostatic controls to the under floor heating downstairs and the radiators upstairs.
  • We use ecologically responsible cleaning products wherever possible.
  • Laundry is line dried in the orchard when the weather permits.
  • We encourage our guests to recycle; and provide appropriate facilities.
  • We are committed to supporting local businesses, and all the services we employ are locally based.
  • We believe in minimising food miles and enjoying the fantastic local produce for which Monmouthshire is so well known. Our welcome pack includes a range of locally produced goodies.
  • We are members of our local community sustainable agriculture project and have recently contributed one of our fields to grow fruit and vegetables collectively.
  • Trevyr used to have a cider mill and our orchard has a range of traditional fruit trees which haven't seen a pesticide for years, if ever. You are welcome to help yourself to fruit in season.
  • There is some fantastic walking and cycling to be enjoyed, and we encourage our guests to get out and enjoy the area.
