Trevyr Barn

Stone built, Trevyr Barn has been restored using traditional materials including stone and oak floors and wooden beams. It is furnished in a comfortable contemporary style to create the perfect holiday accommodation for a short break or longer stay.

Located between the Wye Valley AONB and the Black Mountains, just to the east of the Brecon Beacons National Park, Trevyr Barn is an excellent base for exploring this beautiful and historic area.

This fascinating region of the Welsh borders offers plenty of outdoor activities for all ages and abilities, interesting historical sites and is renowned for its excellent local produce and restaurants.

Grosmont is within easy driving distance of London, Cardiff, the Midlands and Manchester. It is well serviced by local and national train services via Abergavenny, Hereford and Newport (London-Swansea main line).
