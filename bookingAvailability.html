Booking & availability

Prices and availability

Trevyr Barn is available all year round. Prices vary according to the season from £475 to £950 per week. We are also happy to take bookings for short breaks and long weekends. Click here for details of prices and availability.

Changeover day is usually Friday but we can be flexible outside peak times; call or email to check.

Deposits

A booking deposit of 25% will be required to secure your dates, with final payment six weeks prior to arrival.
