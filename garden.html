Garden

Trevyr Barn has a private courtyard with barbeque, as well as a lawned garden looking out onto the orchard.

We have 10 acres of fields, an orchard and a brook. Our guests are welcome to explore, and help themselves to fruit when in season (apples, cherries, pears, plums and blackberries). We have a wealth of wildlife to enjoy and have bird and bat boxes in the orchard to encourage them. Our night skies are pitch black and perfect for star gazing.

There is parking on the property for two cars and secure storage for bicycles and other outdoor equipment.
