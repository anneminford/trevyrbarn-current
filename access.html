Access statement

Local facilities

Trevyr Barn is one mile outside Grosmont village (local shop and post office). The nearest town with supermarket and shopping centre is Abergavenny (9 miles). Abergavenny is on the Manchester to Cardiff train line â€“ regular services are available.

Entrance to barn

A stone-flagged path (very slight uphill gradient) leads from the parking area to the entrance to the barn. Entry is directly through a double door into the sitting room, requiring a small step over the door frame. There is an exterior light by the entrance.

Internal circulation

The downstairs flooring is Travertine stone throughout (underfloor heating, in-room controls). From the sitting room a door leads off into the hallway, from where the utility room and downstairs shower/toilet can be reached (level floor). Two steps lead down though a wide opening into the kitchen/dining room. Oak staircase with handrail on left leads up to the first floor landing. Large kitchen/dining room with table seating 6 (can be extended to 8). Standard height kitchen work tops, drop down oven doors. Stable door leading out to private cobbled courtyard (level threshold, exterior light).

Upstairs layout

Family bathroom and three bedrooms, (two double, one with bunk beds), leading off first floor landing. Level oak floors throughout upstairs. All rooms centrally heated (thermostatic radiators).

Bathrooms and toilets

One downstairs shower room with step-in shower with sliding screen, toilet and wash basin. Upstairs bathroom with toilet, wash basin, bath and handheld shower.

Other

TV, DVD, FM/DAB radio, iPod docking station. Mobile telephone reception is poor locally. Hypo-allergenic pillow and duvets in all bedrooms.

Emergency exits and smoke alarms

Smoke alarms situated in the ground floor hallway, kitchen and upstairs on the landing at the top of the stairwell. Windows in all bedrooms open fully to allow emergency exit. Fire blanket and fire extinguisher in kitchen.

Road access

The barn is situated on a quiet country lane and is approached through a gate leading off the lane.

Parking

There is parking for two cars on a gravel area directly outside the barn, next to the lawned garden.
